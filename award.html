Future Leaders for America Award 2021


Nine high school students from the Lexington Youth STEM Team, guided by Professor Ding Wei, initiated several public welfare projects during the COVID-19 epidemic to serve the Lexington Town community. Their efforts, which spanned all age groups, earned them accolades from the community, a commendation from the Lexington Chinese Association, coverage by the local newspaper Lexington Minuteman, and a letter of commendation from the school district superintendent Dr. Hackett. Their project was also honored by the Civic Leadership Academy jury, winning the joint first prize among over 50 nationwide projects at the 2021 Civic Leadership Institute. Their selfless dedication and professional skills significantly contributed to making Lexington a better place.
